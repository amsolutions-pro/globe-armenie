The 10 major periods of Armenian history

  1. Kingdom of Urartu — c. 860 – 590 BC
  2. Satrapy of Armenia — 590 – 331 BC
  3. Orontid Kingdom — 331 – 200 BC
  4. Artaxiad Kingdom — 189 – 1 BC
  5. Arsacid Kingdom — AD 66 – 428
  6. Marzpanate and Byzantine Armenia — 428 – 640
  7. Arab Arminiya — 640 – 884
  8. Bagratid Kingdoms — 884 – 1045
  9. Cilicia and the Zakarids — 1080 – 1375
  10. Ottoman–Persian partitions — 1514 – 1828
